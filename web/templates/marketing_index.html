Bot Commands

Powerful slash commands to manage your VRChat world showcase

/world-create
Create a new forum channel specifically designed for VRChat worlds with proper permissions and tags.
/world-set
Set up an existing forum channel for VRChat worlds with automatic tagging.
/world
Create a VRChat world submission button in the current channel.
/world-remove
Remove a specific world post by URL, ID, or thread selection.
/scan
Scan the forum for issues and provide options to automatically fix them.
/clean-db
Clean the database by removing references to deleted channels.
/about
Learn what this bot does and how to use it.
/help
Show available commands and their usage.

How To Use

Get started with VRChat World Showcase Bot in just a few simple steps

Add the Bot to Your Server

Click the "Add to Discord" button and select your server. Make sure the bot has proper permissions to manage channels and threads.

Create Your Showcase Channel

Use the /world-create command to set up a new forum channel with proper permissions and tags, or use /world-set to configure an existing forum.

Share with Your Community

Let your community know they can submit VRChat worlds by clicking the "World" button in the pinned post and pasting their world link.

Keep Your Showcase Clean

Periodically use the /scan command to check for issues and fix them automatically. You can also use /world-remove to delete specific world posts.
