Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

Last Updated: March 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the VRChat World Showcase Bot ("Bot") in your Discord server.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Bot, we collect and store the following types of information:

Data Type Purpose Duration Stored
Discord Server ID To identify which server the Bot is operating in Until Bot is removed from server
Discord Channel and Thread IDs To manage forum channels and thread posts Until channels are deleted or the Bot is removed
Discord User IDs To associate world submissions with users Until related world posts are deleted
VRChat World IDs and URLs To display world information and prevent duplicates Until related world posts are deleted
Tag Selections To categorize world posts Until related world posts are deleted

All data is stored in a secure database and is only used for the Bot's functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage VRChat world showcase forums in your Discord server
  • Fetch world information from the VRChat API to populate posts
  • Tag and categorize world posts based on user selections
  • Prevent duplicate world submissions
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the Bot's functionality

We do not use your data for marketing purposes or sell it to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • We may retrieve world information from the VRChat API using world IDs submitted by users
  • We may use basic analytics tools to monitor the Bot's performance and troubleshoot issues
  • If required by law or to protect our rights

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Remove the Bot from your server at any time, which will stop new data collection
  • Request deletion of your server's data by contacting us
  • Use the Bot's built-in commands to manage or delete world posts

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or would like to exercise your rights regarding your data, please contact us through our Discord support server or via email at [contact email].
